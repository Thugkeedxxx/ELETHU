About ELETHU

ELETHU ORGANIZATION (SDPRC) is dedicated to empowering individuals in rural communities by offering essential skills, leadership development, and entrepreneurial training. Our mission is to unlock potential in every participant and create a positive community impact.

Vision: A world where rural communities thrive through skills, leadership, and innovation.

Mission: To provide practical training and mentorship that fosters entrepreneurship and community development.
